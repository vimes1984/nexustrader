# arxiv 2308.09485: ML for Trading Strategies
## Key concepts (from abstract):
- Novel approach to options trading using scalable ML
- Does NOT require specifications of underlying market dynamics
- Does NOT require assumptions on option pricing models
- Directly learns mappings from market data → optimal trading signals
- Backtested on 10+ years of S&P 100 options contracts
- Deep learning end-to-end approach beats rules-based strategies
- Turnover regularization improves performance at high transaction costs
