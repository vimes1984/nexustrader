# Oxford: End-to-End Deep Learning for Options Trading
## Key concepts:
- End-to-end ML: raw market data → trading signals (no intermediate models)
- No Black-Scholes or stochastic process assumptions
- Directly learns non-trivial mappings from market data
- Significant improvements in risk-adjusted performance
- Turnover regularization critical for real-world deployment
- Applicable to any asset class (not just options)
